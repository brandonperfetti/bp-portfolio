## Tips for Being an Effective Mentor

- **Be Approachable:** Create a safe, open environment where your mentee feels comfortable sharing their thoughts, challenges, and successes.
- **Set Clear Objectives:** Help your mentee define clear, achievable goals for your mentorship relationship, and devise a plan to achieve them.

- **Share Your Network:** Introduce your mentee to people in your network who can provide additional guidance, opportunities, and perspectives.
